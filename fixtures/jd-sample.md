# Senior AI Platform Engineer

**Company:** Acme Corp
**Location:** Remote (US)
**Comp:** $220k–$260k base + equity

## About the role

We're hiring a Senior AI Platform Engineer to own the ML platform that powers
our real-time fraud detection and personalization stack. You'll work across
Kafka, feature stores, and model serving, and partner with applied ML teams
to cut deployment time from weeks to hours.

## What you'll do

- Lead the platform team building model registry, feature store, A/B testing
  framework, and inference serving infrastructure.
- Drive Kafka-based event pipelines that move features and predictions across
  fraud detection and recommendation surfaces.
- Cut model deployment latency: invest in CI/CD, automated rollback, and
  drift monitoring so deploys move from weeks to hours.
- Mentor 2–3 ML engineers and set the technical bar for the org.

## What we're looking for

- 5+ years building production ML platforms (model registry, feature store,
  serving, monitoring).
- Strong Kafka / event-streaming background. Bonus: Kubernetes, SageMaker.
- Track record of measurable platform impact (deploy time, model count,
  latency).
- Comfortable owning end-to-end systems with on-call rotation.

## Keywords

ML platform, MLOps, Kafka, feature store, model registry, SageMaker, drift
detection, A/B testing, real-time inference, fraud detection.
